Penguin Motion

What's penguin motion called?

The walking motion when penguins are on the move from one location to another is called waddle. Penguins have short legs and their knees are close to their body. This makes the penguin walk look like a waddle, and hence the name. The next paragraph contains some very important information about penguins.
